Possessive adjectives show ownership. They tell us who something belongs to and are always followed by a noun.

🎯 Key Rule

They act as adjectives, modifying a noun. They cannot stand alone.

✅ My jacket is red.
❌ The jacket is my.

👫 Agreement Rule

The adjective agrees with the owner, not the object(s) owned.

👩 Her book (1 object)
👩 Her books (2+ objects)
