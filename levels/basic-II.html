Basic II

The student is able to understand and use everyday expressions and very basic phrases aimed at satisfying immediate needs. They can introduce themselves and others, ask for and give basic personal information about their home, belongings, and people they know. They can interact in a simple way provided the other person speaks slowly and clearly and is prepared to help.
