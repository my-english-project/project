BASIC LEVELS (A1 - A2)

LEVEL A1 (Basic I & II)

The student is able to understand and use everyday expressions and very basic phrases aimed at satisfying immediate needs. They can introduce themselves and others, ask for and give basic personal information...

LEVEL A2 (Basic III & IV)

The student is able to understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevance. They can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a simple and direct exchange of information...
